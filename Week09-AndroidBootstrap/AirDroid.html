About AirDroid

AirDroid lets you use and manage your Android device over the air, using only a Web browser. Itâ€™s a wire-free solution for those who hate having to switch between computer and tablet frequently.

All that you need to be able to use AirDroid is the following:

  1. Download and Install AirDroid to your tablet for free from the Play Store.
  2. A desktop computer, notebook, or netbook with a Web browser that is compliant (most current browsers are).
  3. Wireless LAN connectivity.

Its that easy -- Download and install to your device, connect to the device through your PC's browser by navigating to the web URL shown on the device. Once you are there you can scan the QR code and it connects once its verified. After you are connected you can upload/download files, monitor activities, and even send text messages if your mobile device has a service provider associated to it.
